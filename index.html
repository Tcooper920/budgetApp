How to Create a Budget
  1. Enter your monthly income amount.
  2. Enter your monthly expenses: Enter the expense name, enter the dollar amount, and select an expense type from the dropdown. (Expenses can be sorted by selecting an option from the Sort Expenses dropdown). Expenses can be deleted by clicking the âœ• next to each Expenses list item.
  3. The graph will show a breakdown of your spending by expense type. Underneath the graph, you will see how much money you have left to budget with.
  4. When you have no more money left to budget with, you can export your budget by clicking the Print/Export Budget button at the top.
